Creating a maze:
* When starting the scene, you will see two sliders for height and width;
* Minimum values ​​for maze 3 x 3
* Then click on the Play button
* During the game, you can press f1 to bring up the settings window and change the values ​​for the maze

Player control:
W = up
A = left
S = back
D = right

Camera control:
* Move your mouse pointer up to zoom in on the camera, or move down to zoom out.
